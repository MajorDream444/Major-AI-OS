# A Awareness — Shot A Remotion Notes

Opening cinematic hook for A = Awareness.

This shot should function as a reusable background layer or opening scene.

- Gold letter A
- `A IS FOR AWARENESS`
- `Awareness comes before automation.`

No aggressive transitions.
