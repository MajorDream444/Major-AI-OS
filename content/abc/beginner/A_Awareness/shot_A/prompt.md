# A Awareness — Shot A Prompt

## Shot Title

Invisible Weight

## Duration

10 seconds

## Purpose

Emotional hook. Scroll stopper. Make the viewer recognize the hidden burden of repeated tasks before introducing AI as a solution.

## Prompt

Use the attached reference images as canonical references for the Major AI Mindset ABC series. Preserve the exact facial features, skin tone, calm expression, and teacher presence of Major Dream Williams from the portrait reference. Use the MAIM logo as the official brand mark where appropriate. Match the A-Z Roadmap visual identity: black foundation, gold authority, Jamaican green accents, champion blue highlights, minimal typography, warm cinematic lighting, luxury editorial composition, and clean negative space.

10-second cinematic vertical video, 9:16. A modern city street at golden hour. Major Dream Williams walks calmly through the city wearing the same dark tailored jacket from the reference image, with the MAIM woven crown symbol subtly present as a medallion or visual motif. Around him, invisible glowing backpacks slowly appear, each made of calendars, clocks, emails, notifications, sticky notes, invoices, and unfinished projects. The backpacks are symbolic and elegant, not cartoonish. As more hidden responsibilities appear, the world around him keeps moving normally. Major pauses with awareness, not panic. Slow cinematic push-in. Warm documentary realism. No robots, no dystopian tech, no chaotic sci-fi. Final frame: a glowing gold letter A appears in clean editorial typography over a black background with subtle gold corner framing.

## Must Feel Like

The viewer thinks: that is me.

## Avoid

- scary robots
- messy blue hologram overload
- generic corporate stock footage
- exaggerated panic
- cheap sci-fi
- text-heavy frames

## End Frame

Gold letter A on black background.
