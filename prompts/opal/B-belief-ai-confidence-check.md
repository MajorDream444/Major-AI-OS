# Google Opal Prompt — B is for Belief

## App Name

AI Confidence Check

## Series

Major AI Mindset ABCs

## Letter

B = Belief

## Purpose

Build an interactive mini-app that helps beginners identify what belief is blocking them from using AI and gives them one safe first experiment.

## Prompt

Build an interactive mini-app called **AI Confidence Check** for the Major AI Mindset ABC Studio.

This app is based on:

**B = Belief**

Big idea:

**If you can text, you can use AI.**

The app should help users realize that AI is accessible even if they are not technical.

## Audience

This app is for:

- Beginners curious about AI
- People who feel AI is too complicated
- Small business owners
- Coaches
- Consultants
- Community leaders
- Students
- People who say “I’m not technical”

Tone:

- Calm
- Clear
- Beginner-safe
- Encouraging
- Practical

Avoid:

- Hype
- Shame
- Technical jargon
- Making the user feel behind

## App Flow

### Screen 1 — Welcome

Title:

**AI Confidence Check**

Subtitle:

If you can text, you can use AI.

Body:

This short check will help you see what is stopping you from starting and give you one simple AI task to try today.

Button:

**Start Check**

### Screen 2 — Comfort Level

Question:

How comfortable do you feel using AI right now?

Options:

- I have never used it
- I tried it once or twice
- I use it sometimes
- I use it often but still feel scattered
- I feel confident

### Screen 3 — Belief Blocker

Question:

What belief blocks you the most?

Options:

- I am not technical
- I do not know what to ask
- I am afraid of looking foolish
- I do not trust the answers
- I do not have time to learn
- I do not see how it applies to me
- Other

### Screen 4 — First Useful Task

Question:

What is one simple thing you wish AI could help you with this week?

Input type:

Long text

Helper text:

Examples: rewrite a message, summarize notes, organize ideas, draft a caption, explain something simply.

### Screen 5 — Confidence Trigger

Question:

What would make you feel confident enough to try?

Options:

- A simple example
- A step-by-step guide
- Someone showing me live
- A template I can copy
- A safe practice task
- A community learning with me

### Screen 6 — Score Calculation

Generate an **AI Confidence Score** from 0 to 100.

Suggested scoring:

- Has used AI before = +20
- Can name belief blocker = +20
- Can name one useful task = +25
- Can name confidence trigger = +20
- Willing to try this week = +15

Score levels:

0–30: **Starting From Zero**

Message:

You are not behind. You only need one safe first step.

31–60: **Curious But Cautious**

Message:

You are closer than you think. One simple task can build your confidence.

61–85: **Ready To Practice**

Message:

You have enough belief to start. Now you need repetition.

86–100: **Confidence In Motion**

Message:

You are ready to turn AI into a regular part of your workflow.

### Screen 7 — Results

Show:

1. AI Confidence Score
2. Confidence Level
3. Belief Blocker
4. First Safe AI Task
5. 5-Minute Action
6. CTA

5-Minute Action:

Open an AI chat tool and ask it to help you rewrite one message, email, or idea you already have.

CTA:

Comment **BELIEF** or join the Major AI Mindset Beginner Track.

Buttons:

- Copy My Results
- Join Major AI Mindset
