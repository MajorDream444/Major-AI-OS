No robots.

No sci-fi overload.

Just humans becoming more confident.

## Storyboard Direction

### Opening

Golden sunrise.

A little girl opens a notebook.

A grandmother looks at a phone with curiosity.

A young entrepreneur unlocks a shop.

A teacher opens classroom doors.

Major Dream walks through the scene wearing his signature woven palm hat.

People notice him, not like a celebrity, but like a trusted guide.

### Verse

Show small, emotional learning moments:

- A teenager helping his father
- A barber asking AI a question
- A market vendor smiling after solving a problem
- A single mother learning on a laptop while her daughter colors beside her
- A retired athlete recording his story

Feeling:

> Everyone starts somewhere.

### Pre-Chorus

People begin helping one another.

Hands reaching.

Phones shared.

Smiles.

Children teaching grandparents.

Friends laughing after discovering something together.

The camera slowly circles around them.

### Chorus

Everything opens up.

Children run through a colorful community.

Different cultures appear naturally:

- Caribbean
- African
- Asian
- Latino
- Middle Eastern
- European

No stereotypes.

Just humanity.

As the children’s choir enters, beautiful animated glowing letters appear:

- A
- B
- C

Each letter briefly transforms into real-world scenes:

- A = Awareness
- B = Belief
- C = Context

The world literally becomes brighter.

### Bridge

Emotional transformation moments:

- A grandfather reads a story to his grandson
- A woman launches her first online business
- A teenager proudly helps his mother
- A classroom applauds
- Major quietly watches from the background

The hero is not Major.

The hero is the people.

### Final Chorus

Everyone comes together in a huge outdoor community celebration.

Music.

Dance.

Children.

Families.

Entrepreneurs.

Teachers.

Artists.

Athletes.

The camera slowly rises into the sky.

Thousands of glowing letters drift upward.

Not just A, B, C.

The entire alphabet.

A symbol that learning never ends.

Final image:

Major looks toward the sunrise as children run ahead of him.

Text:
