## Weekly Webinar Role

The webinar is not a one-time launch event.

It is a weekly 60-minute grounding room for the community.

The first webinar should introduce the founder, the movement, and the first three letters:

```txt
A = Awareness
B = Belief
C = Context
```

The goal of Week 1 is not to impress people with complexity.

The goal is to make AI feel safe, human, and useful.

If someone leaves saying, “For the first time, AI does not feel scary,” the first webinar worked.

## First Five Letters

### A — Awareness

Big idea:

```txt
You cannot improve what you have not noticed.
```

Beginner lesson:

Awareness is the moment you stop reacting and start observing. Before AI can help, you need to notice where your time, energy, attention, or creativity is leaking.

Five-minute action:

```txt
For the next 24 hours, write down every moment you think: “I do this all the time.”
```

Community question:

```txt
What is one task you repeat every single week?
```

### B — Belief

Big idea:

```txt
If you can text, you can use AI.
```

Beginner lesson:

Belief comes before skill. Most beginners do not need more tools first. They need to believe they are allowed to begin.

Five-minute action:

```txt
Ask ChatGPT one question about a real problem in your life or business.
```

Community question:

```txt
What is one thing you thought was too technical for you before today?
```

### C — Context

Big idea:

```txt
The quality of your output depends on the quality of your input.
```

Beginner lesson:

AI does not read your mind. It responds to the context you give it. Better context creates better answers, and better answers build confidence.

Five-minute action:

```txt
Ask the same question twice: once with one sentence, then again with full context.
```

Community question:

```txt
What details did AI miss when your prompt was too vague?
```

### D — Direction

Big idea:

```txt
Know where you are going before asking AI to help.
```

Beginner lesson:

AI can move fast, but speed without direction creates noise. Before you ask for help, name the outcome.

Five-minute action:

```txt
Write one sentence that starts with: “I want AI to help me...”
```

Community question:

```txt
What result are you trying to create this week?
```

### E — Experiment

Big idea:

```txt
You do not have to master AI. You have to start.
```

Beginner lesson:

Experiment means you are allowed to try, adjust, and learn without shame. The first prompt does not need to be perfect. It only needs to begin the conversation.

Five-minute action:

```txt
Try one small AI task today and write down what worked and what did not.
```

Community question:

```txt
What did you try, and what did you learn?
```

## Design Rules

Always reference `MAIM 00 Brand System` before producing visuals.

Carry forward:

- Black foundation
- Gold authority
- Jamaican green legacy
- Knicks blue/orange champion energy
- White clarity
- Crown seal / palm crown symbolism
- Human-led, AI-amplified message
- Beginner-safe emotional tone

Avoid:

- Generic robots
- Cold sci-fi
- SaaS stock-image energy
- Overcrowded text
- Fear-based AI messaging
- Making AI the hero

Humans are the hero.
AI is the amplifier.
Culture is the memory.
Major is the translator.
