# Google AI Studio One Shot Prompt — MAIM Mission Control Landing Page

## Purpose

Use this prompt in Google AI Studio to quickly generate a visual prototype before spending Codex or implementation time.

This is for exploration only.

Do not treat the output as production code until reviewed.

## Model Use

Use Gemini in Google AI Studio as the first design pass.

Goal:

Create a high fidelity single page landing prototype for Major AI Mindset that combines the emotional portal feeling of the Gravity Well concept with the practical clarity of the Structural Landing concept.

## Prompt

You are designing the first public landing page for Major AI Mindset.

Major AI Mindset is not a generic AI course.

It is the mindset operating system for the AI age.

It helps beginners, builders, creators, entrepreneurs, parents, professionals, athletes, coaches, and community leaders understand AI, use it with confidence, and build real digital assets one step at a time.

The page should feel like a doorway into a universe of learning, but it must still be simple, fast, readable, and conversion focused.

Use a hybrid structure.

The opening hero should feel like a portal into the MAIM universe.

Below the hero, the page should become a clean luxury editorial landing page with practical sections that explain the offer clearly.

Do not make the whole page complicated.

Do not make it look like a generic SaaS website.

Do not make it look overly futuristic or robotic.

The hero may use abstract universe, orbit, gravity, glowing spheres, or roadmap energy, but the rest of the page must feel grounded, human, premium, and clear.
