# MAIM Structural Landing Page Experiment

Status: Experiment
Owner: Major Dream Williams
Branch: feature/maim-gravity-well-landing-test

## Purpose

This experiment tests a simpler, luxury editorial landing page structure for Major AI Mindset.

It is inspired by the NEXT Founder Cohort layout, but adapted for MAIM.

The goal is to create a practical conversion page for live knowledge sessions, discovery calls, and future cohort entry.

## Strategic Read

The Gravity Well prototype gives us the portal.

This structural landing page gives us the clarity.

Together, they can become one system:

- Gravity Well = emotional universe entry
- Structural Landing = practical explanation and conversion

This page should feel premium, grounded, calm, and clear.

It should not feel like a generic AI landing page.

## Why This Layout Works

The NEXT-style layout has strong bones:

- Sticky navigation
- Clear hero statement
- Facts at a glance
- Founder/architect section
- Methodology explanation
- Day-by-day or session-by-session breakdown
- Investment/application section
- FAQ
- Final CTA

For MAIM, those sections become:

- The Translator
- The Methodology
- The Knowledge Sessions
- The ABC Roadmap
- The Community
- Apply / Register

## MAIM Adaptation

Replace the cohort-only frame with a broader MAIM funnel:

1. Free live knowledge sessions
2. Discovery calls
3. Beginner lab
4. Monthly digital asset lab
5. Cohort / implementation offer

## Decision Rule

This version should be the practical public landing page candidate.

The Gravity Well version should remain the premium hero candidate.

Do not merge either into production until both are compared against:

- Clarity
- Speed
- Mobile usability
- Signup conversion
- Brand fit

## Technical Boundary

This version should be lightweight.

No Three.js.

No heavy animation.

No unnecessary third-party scripts beyond Tailwind, Iconify, and fonts for prototype phase.

This gives us a clean baseline against the Gravity Well version.
