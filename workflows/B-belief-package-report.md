## Core Concept

**B = Belief**

Subtheme: **Basics**

## Big Idea

If you can text, you can use AI.

## Lesson

Most people think AI is too complicated because they believe they need to understand the technology before they can use it. That is not true. Beginners do not need to understand every model, setting, or technical term. They need belief that AI is accessible, and they need one simple problem to practice with.

Belief comes before skill. The first win is realizing that talking to AI can be as simple as sending a clear message.

## 5-Minute Action

Open an AI chat tool and ask it to help you rewrite one message, email, or idea you already have.
