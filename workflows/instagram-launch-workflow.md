# MAIM Instagram Launch Workflow

Account: `@major_ai_mindset`

## Operating Principle

Instagram is the front door. The goal is not to look busy. The goal is to move the right people from attention into action.

## Launch Sequence

### Phase 1 — Account Setup

1. Confirm profile photo uses the MAIM logo.
2. Set name field: `Major AI Mindset | AI for Builders`.
3. Set bio using the launch playbook.
4. Put one intake link in bio.
5. Create highlights: Start Here, A-Z AI, Webinar, Tools, Wins, Skool.

### Phase 2 — First 9 Posts

1. What is Major AI Mindset?
2. A is for Awareness
3. Free Webinar Announcement
4. B is for Belief
5. Why AI is not just for tech people
6. C is for Context
7. The A-Z Roadmap
8. 3 things AI can help you do this week
9. Founder post: why Major is building MAIM

### Phase 3 — Pin + Route

Pin:

1. What is MAIM?
2. A-Z Roadmap
3. Webinar

Route every CTA to one of these:

- Comment MAIM
- Comment A-Z
- Comment BELIEF
- Comment BUILD
- Tap link in bio

## Daily Publishing Rhythm

Minimum viable daily rhythm:

1. One ABC or practical AI post.
2. One Story pointing to the intake form or webinar.
3. Check comments and DMs.
4. Capture lead signals.
5. Update Airtable / tracker status.

## Weekly Rhythm

Monday: ABC lesson
Tuesday: Real-life AI use case
Wednesday: Build With Me
Thursday: Mindset + Legacy
Friday: Webinar / cohort CTA
Saturday: Story recap / community prompt
Sunday: Live, webinar, reflection, or founder note

## Status Logic

`Draft` = idea exists.

`Ready to Publish` = copy and visual are good enough to go live.

`Published` = live on Instagram.

`Post-Publish Completion` = needs archive, repurposing, transcript, or metadata cleanup.

`Complete` = full asset package stored and tracked.

Do not block publishing because the archive package is incomplete.

## Operator Checklist

For every post:

- Hook is clear.
- Visual is readable.
- Caption sounds human.
- CTA is simple.
- Asset is tracked.
- Comments are monitored.
- Learning signals are logged.

## Learning Signals

Track:

- Saves
- Shares
- Comments that show understanding
- DMs asking for roadmap, webinar, or cohort
- People saying they are beginners
- People asking for help with a specific business or idea

## Definition of Done for Launch

The launch is complete when:

1. Bio is updated.
2. Intake link is active.
3. First 9 posts are drafted or scheduled.
4. First 3 pins are live.
5. Highlights are created.
6. Cowork has daily reporting instructions.
7. Codex has implementation tasks.
8. Claude Code has review criteria.
