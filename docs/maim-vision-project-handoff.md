# Major AI Mindset (MAIM) — Vision & Project Handoff

Version: June 2026

## Executive Summary

Major AI Mindset (MAIM) is an AI education and digital asset ecosystem founded by Major Dream Williams.

It is not simply a course, webinar, or content brand.

Its purpose is to become the translation layer between advanced AI technology and everyday people.

The mission is simple:

Help people move from overwhelm to confidence.

Our belief is that AI does not need more experts.

It needs more translators.

We exist to help entrepreneurs, creators, athletes, parents, church leaders, educators, retirees, and business owners confidently use AI to build assets, businesses, and legacies.

Our guiding principle is:

> If you can text, you can use AI.

Our cultural mantra is:

> Likkle by likkle, we build the future.

## The ABC Framework

The ABCs of AI Mindset are the curriculum spine.

Every letter teaches:

- One idea
- One lesson
- One action

Current foundation:

| Letter | Concept | Beginner Line |
| --- | --- | --- |
| A | Awareness | Notice before you automate. |
| B | Belief | If you can text, you can use AI. |
| C | Context | The quality of your output depends on the quality of your input. |
| D | Direction | Know where you’re going before asking AI to help. |
| E | Experiment | Progress belongs to those willing to begin. |

The remaining letters continue this pattern through Z.
