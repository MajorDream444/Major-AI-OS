Status: Locked

Source: Claude Design feedback, ChatGPT strategy thread, uploaded screenshots, and MAIM YouTube identity board.

## What This Is

MAIM 04 is the reusable YouTube broadcast identity for Major AI Mindset.

It is built from the MAIM 00 Brand System:

- Black foundation
- Gold authority
- Green · gold · blue tri-bar
- Archivo headline type
- JetBrains Mono utility/spec type
- Crown coin seal as the trust mark
- Human-first AI education positioning

This system turns the Major AI Mindset YouTube channel into a recognizable broadcast package instead of random thumbnails.

The guiding principle is simple:

> Every thumbnail must read in under 2 seconds on a phone.

If the promise is not clear in two seconds, cut words, increase type size, or simplify the composition.

## Pre-Publish Checklist

Before any thumbnail or cover ships:

- Promise readable in under 2 seconds on a phone
- Max 4–5 words of headline when possible
- No paragraphs
- Crown seal or MD mark present on a solid patch
- Tri-bar under the headline
- One accent color only
- Real human in frame where it fits
- Humans are the hero
- File named correctly

Naming convention:

```txt
maim_yt_[topic]_1280x720_v#
```

For A-B tested winners:

```txt
maim_yt_[topic]_1280x720_v#_win
```
