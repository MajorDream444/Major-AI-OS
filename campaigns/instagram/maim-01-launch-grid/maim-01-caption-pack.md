# Post 06 — C is for Context

**Image file:** `06-maim-c-context.png`

**Caption:**

C is for Context.

Better input means better output.

AI does not read your mind.

It responds to what you give it.

If you ask a vague question, you usually get a vague answer.

If you give context, you get something useful.

Instead of asking:

“Help me make content.”

Try:

“I am a wellness coach helping busy mothers. I want to make one Instagram post that explains how AI can help them plan their week. Keep it simple, warm, and practical.”

That is context.

Today’s 5-minute action:

Ask the same question twice:

Once with little context.
Once with full context.

Compare the answers.
