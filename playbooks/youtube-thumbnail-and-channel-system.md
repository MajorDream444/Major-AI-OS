## Thumbnail Principles

Every thumbnail must do three things:

1. Earn attention
2. Communicate the promise
3. Preserve MAIM visual identity

The thumbnail earns the click.

The video earns the trust.

The Vault owns the asset.

## Thumbnail Formula

```txt
Human face or strong symbol
+
Huge readable promise
+
MAIM color doctrine
+
Crown seal / mark
+
Minimal supporting text
```

## Design Standards

Use:

- 1280 x 720 for YouTube thumbnails
- High contrast text
- Large readable words
- One primary idea only
- Founder face when building trust
- Logo / seal as stamp of authority
- Mobile-first readability

Avoid:

- too many words
- crowded scenes
- tiny subtitles
- generic robot graphics
- low-contrast gold-on-black overload
- too many icons

## Title + Thumbnail Pairing

The title and thumbnail should not repeat the exact same job.

The thumbnail should create the emotional hook.

The title should complete the search / curiosity promise.

Example:

Thumbnail:

```txt
THE FUTURE NEEDS A TRANSLATOR
```

Title:

```txt
AI Needs a Translator — Why Beginners Are Not Too Late
```
